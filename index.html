Sabira Ranjit

GM @ HR Factory "Your Better Half."

Sabira Ranjit is a Human Resources (HR) and Learning & Development (L&D) Professional with over 12 years of experience at national and international levels. She holds a master’s degree in Tourism and is currently pursuing a PhD in Psychology.
With extensive experience in human resources and learning & development across various prestigious establishments, she currently serves as the Assistant Director of HR and L&D at Aarya Hotel & Spa. She was a Manager at Ramada Encore by Wyndham in Thamel, Kathmandu earlier. In this role, she had overseen HR functions and learning initiatives, driving organizational excellence and employee development.
Her career began as HR coordination at Radisson Blu Resort, Sharjah, where she honed my skills in HR management. She, then advanced to roles with increasing responsibilities at Jumeirah Creekside Hotel and Kempinski Mall of Emirates, where she focused on diverse aspects of HR, including housing management.
In Kathmandu, she further expanded her expertise as a Corporate HR Manager at Kathmandu Guest House, managing HR operations across multiple properties, and as an HR Consultant for the launch of Hard Rock Café in Durbar Marg. These roles refined my ability to manage complex HR setups and consult on strategic initiatives.
